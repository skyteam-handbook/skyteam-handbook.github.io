Music Bot User Handbook

A simple guide to playing music in your server

Getting Started

The bot plays music from YouTube using simple commands. Make sure the bot is in a voice channel before using music commands.

Joining & Leaving

+join — Makes the bot join your current voice channel.

+leave — Disconnects the bot and clears the queue.

Playing Music

+play <song name or URL>

  • Plays a YouTube song by name or link.
  • If music already plays, the song is added to the queue.
  • Example: +play feel good inc

Playback Controls

+pause — Pauses the current song.

+resume — Resumes paused music.

+skip — Skips to the next song in the queue.

Viewing the Queue

+queue

  • Shows all songs currently waiting to play.
  • Shows the now-playing track.

Tips

  • You can paste full YouTube URLs.
  • You can search songs by name.
  • Skipping automatically starts the next track if one exists.
